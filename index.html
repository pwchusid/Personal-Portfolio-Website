About

Well, Hello There! My name is Peter Chusid. I am a former paramedic turned website developer. I have experience with a variety of frameworks, scripting, and mark-up languages and I would be thrilled for the opportunity to develop your next responsive, powerful website.

Feel free to browse my projects, skills declarations, and services sections and then send over your information so we can talk. Additionally, click below for more about me.

Greetings from Connecticut! I graduated from Quinnipiac University in 2017 with a Bachelors in Sociology. I completed my degree and very shortly thereafter began a stint as an EMT in Connecticut. I followed this up with advanced practice training as a Paramedic which I have been for the past year. However, I've always felt a greater calling, in particular to the field of Front-End Development.

After building my own PC and following closely the rapid and exciting expansion of the tech industry, I started taking classes on Code Academy, and the rest is history! My training in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, alongside other frameworks and asssociated programming training has allowed me to enjoy significant progress which can be shown below. I'd be thrilled to have a part in developing and maintaining one of the many sites users view around the world.
